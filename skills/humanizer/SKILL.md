# Humanizer

## Agent compatibility

Use the tools available in the current agent environment. Follow the current agent's sandbox, approval, and file-editing rules. When editing a file, preserve its format and show the user what changed.

## Required procedure

1. Determine the language of the requested output.
2. For Chinese output, read [the complete Chinese guide](references/chinese-guide.md). For other languages, read [the complete upstream guide](references/upstream-guide.md).
3. Preserve facts, intent, audience, and requested tone. Do not invent personal experiences, named sources, quotations, studies, or specific details merely to make text sound human.
4. Follow the selected guide's review and rewriting process, then perform its final self-check before returning or applying the result.
